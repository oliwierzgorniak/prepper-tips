NATURAL BUNKER

Wanna own a bunker, but don’t want to spend so much money on an ordinary one. Find a cave near your place of living and turn it into your own natural bunker. Now you can be safe with your friends when a disaster comes. If you want to scare away other people from your bunker put a sign nearby informing that a hungry grizzly lives inside the cave.

CARING POLISH GRANDMA

Your budget can’t handle getting more food for a disaster? Ask a polish grandma if she wants to join you during such hard times. Polish grandmas are known for taking care of their grandchildren eating, so you won’t be starving. You might even gain some weight :)

WE SAY NO TO MIND CONTROL

Have you recently thought about helping your friends avoiding being mind controlled by the government? With my tip you can do it easily and cheaply. Collect a tin foil after they have lunch. Next when folding tin foil into hats put some garlic inside. Not only it will protect you from the government, but from aliens as well. Handle such hats to your baddies. They won’t stop thanking you.

SHOOTING ON A BUDGET

Every time you go to a shooting rage you loose a fortune? Now you can pay a friction of that. Next time you go to a shooting rage take a toy gun with you. Practice precision with foam bullets and show off the color of your weapon. If you don't have a toy gun you may use a banana as well.

AN INVISIBLE CAR

Do you want in a cheap way camouflage your car and you live in Belgium? There is a big chance that brick camouflage will work, as in the cities you can find them everywhere. Take used bricks and stick them to your car. Be sure to mark the location of it on a map, as you might have problems finding it.
